Privacy Policy  ·  Effective Date: April 18, 2026

Thank you for using PeakPicks. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app. By using PeakPicks, you agree to the practices described below.

1 Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your email address and the display name you choose. This is stored securely through Google Firebase Authentication.

Tier List Data

The tier lists, rankings, and item details (names, descriptions, rankings) you create are saved to Google Cloud Firestore and associated with your account.

Photos and Images

Photos you add to tier list items are stored locally on your device only. They are never uploaded to our servers or any cloud storage.

Usage Data

We do not collect analytics, crash reports, advertising identifiers, or behavioral tracking data of any kind.

2 How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to:

  • Create and maintain your PeakPicks account
  • Save and sync your tier lists across app sessions
  • Authenticate you when you sign in
  • Allow account recovery via your email address

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

3 Third-Party Services

PeakPicks uses the following Google Firebase services:

  • Firebase Authentication — manages account sign-in and security
  • Cloud Firestore — stores your tier list data in the cloud

These services are operated by Google LLC and governed by Google's Privacy Policy. We do not use any advertising SDKs or analytics platforms.

4 Data Storage and Security

Your credentials are secured through Firebase Authentication using industry-standard encryption. Your Firestore data is protected by security rules that ensure only you can read or modify your own tier lists.

Photos are stored in the app's private documents directory on your device and are not accessible to other apps or users.

5 Data Retention and Deletion

Your tier list data is retained in Firestore for as long as your account exists. Locally stored photos remain on your device until you delete them within the App or uninstall it.

To delete your account and all associated data, contact us at the email in Section 9. We will permanently remove everything within 30 days of your request.

6 Children's Privacy

PeakPicks is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal information, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7 Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate account information via the Profile screen
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Withdraw consent at any time by deleting your account

8 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the Effective Date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
