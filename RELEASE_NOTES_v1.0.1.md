# PeakPicks v1.0.1 — Release Notes

Three options below. Pick the one that best matches your tone, then paste the
contents (including the `<en-US>` tags) into the Release notes box on the Play
Console.

Google Play limits release notes to **500 characters per language** — all three
options below are well within that limit.

---

## Option 1 — Detailed (recommended)

```
<en-US>
What's new in PeakPicks v1.0.1:

• Interactive walkthrough greets new users with a quick tour of every ranking style
• New "Rate Your App" button — share your thoughts right from your Profile
• Send Feedback option lets you report bugs and suggest features in one tap
• Replay the walkthrough anytime from your Profile
• Smoother guest sign-in with clearer error messages
• UX polish across the app

Thanks to our closed testers — keep the feedback coming!
</en-US>
```
