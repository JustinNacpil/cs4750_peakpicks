## ASO Notes

- **Title**: includes the highest-volume keyword "Tier List Maker" right after
  the brand name.
- **Short description**: leads with the verb users actually search ("Rank
  anything"), follows with synonyms ("tier lists", "brackets", "rankings").
- **Full description**: front-loads benefits, uses bullet stars (★) to break up
  text and improve scannability, includes a dedicated keyword block at the
  bottom for indexing without keyword-stuffing the prose.
- **Long-tail keywords** ("March Madness bracket", "top 10 list", "tournament
  bracket") capture niche search intent.
- **Use-case section** ("Perfect for…") helps the Play Store algorithm match
  the app to interest categories.
